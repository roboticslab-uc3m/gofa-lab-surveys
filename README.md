# gofa-lab-surveys

Collection of surveys and tools for laboratory sessions with ABB CRB 15000-5 "GoFa" robots at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

![GoFa robot drawing](fig/gofa-pen-real.png)

The survey results presented here led to the following research article:

Bartek Łukawski, Edwin D. Oña, Juan G. Victores, Carlos Balaguer, and Alberto Jardón. Assessing Student Satisfaction and System Usability in Industrial Robotics Education. In *II Simposio CEA de los GT: Ingeniería
de Control – Modelado, Simulación y Optimización – Educación en Automática (SIME)*, pages xx–xx. 2026.
